MEDICINES

Medicines are used to prevent, treat, or manage diseases and health conditions. some of the medicines are:

1. PARACETAMOL

Paracetamol used for the pain relief and fever reduction

SIDE EFFECTS:

  1. Common side effects: Nausea, vomiting, and constipation.
  2. Rare but serious effects: Liver damage, kidney issues, and blood disorders if taken in excessive amounts.
  3. Allergic reactions: Rash, swelling, and low blood pressure.
  4. Overdose symptoms: Diarrhea, loss of appetite, stomach pain, and yellowing of the skin (jaundice).

2.Insulin

Insulin Essential for managing diabetes.

SIDE EFFECTS:

  1. Hypoglycemia (Low Blood Sugar) : Symptoms include dizziness, sweating, confusion, and rapid heartbeat.
  2. Weight Gain : Insulin can lead to increased fat storage.
  3. Injection Site Reactions : Redness, swelling, or pain at the injection site.
